Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about CiboCocinar.

Do I need to install an app?

No! CiboCocinar works directly in your browser. You can access it from any device with a microphone and internet connection. No downloads or installations required.

What languages are supported?

Currently, we support English, Spanish, French, German, and Italian. More languages are coming soon! The AI understands natural language in these languages and can respond appropriately.

Is there a free version?

Yes! Our free plan includes up to 10 recipe generations per month and basic voice guidance. Premium plans offer unlimited recipes, advanced features, and priority support. You can start for free with no credit card required.

Can I use it on my smartphone?

Absolutely! CiboCocinar is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and computers. The interface adapts to your screen size, making it easy to use while cooking in your kitchen.

How does the AI understand my ingredients?

Our AI uses natural language processing to understand what ingredients you have, their quantities, and their conditions. It can suggest substitutions and create recipes based on what's available in your kitchen.

Is my voice data stored?

No, we follow a strict privacy-first approach. Voice data is processed instantly and never stored. Your cooking habits and preferences remain completely private and are not tracked or shared.
